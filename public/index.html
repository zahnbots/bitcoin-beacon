The Take · Opinion

Left and Right Agree: Keep the State Out of Your Wallet

A new national survey found rare bipartisan agreement: Americans across the spectrum don't want the government watching their money. The right fears the state; the left fears creditors and platforms — same demand, opposite doors. Self-custodied bitcoin is the tool that answers it. The honest caveat: bitcoin's base layer is pseudonymous, not private, and self-custody is a responsibility, not a default. The point isn't guaranteed anonymity. It's the option to opt out.
